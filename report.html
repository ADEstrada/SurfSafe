Safety Tips

  • •
    Flag Awareness: Always check the color of the flags at the lifeguard towers before entering the water.
  • •
    Rip Current Strategy: If caught in an offshore current, do not swim against it. Swim parallel to the shore until you are out of the current.
  • •
    Buddy System: Never surf or swim alone. Always have a companion watching out for you.
  • •
    Stay Hydrated: The tropical sun can be intense; drink plenty of water and use reef-safe sunscreen.
  • •
    Respect the Locals: Follow the surfing etiquette and respect the local community and marine life.
